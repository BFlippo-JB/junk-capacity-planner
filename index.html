Dynamic Forecast

Editable live forecast. Each cell shows BC live demand merged with your baseline; your overrides turn yellow. Drives Executive Snapshot, Daily Planner, and Weekly Summary.
Merge order per cell: your override ← BC live demand ← baseline forecast ← 0. Clear an override by pressing Reset on that cell, or "Clear all overrides" to fall back to BC + baseline.
